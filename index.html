ABOUT

Q-Wissenaire is a national-level competitive exam for students in grades 6th to 12th, aiming to bring about a qualitative improvement in the problem-solving skills of students in the country. The exam provides an opportunity for JEE aspirants to compare and compete with other peers across the country. We seek to provide the students with a platform to interact with students at IIT and to ac- quire fundamental experience and knowledge through out-of-the-box thinking.
